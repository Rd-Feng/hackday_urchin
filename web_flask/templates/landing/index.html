Unitube

UniTube is an online service that allows users to view a live feed of the comments on any Youtube video. The service is completely free and is ideal for those who want a more community-minded experience when watching YouTube videos. Because it also retrieves various other metrics about a video, it is also great for people without the time to search various sites to retrieve data about a YouTube video.
